
### `Definition`

**Software Engineering** is the discipline focused on the **structured creation, testing, and maintenance of software systems**.  

It applies **engineering principles** to software development to ensure solutions are **reliable, scalable, maintainable, and efficient**.

---
### `Key Areas`

- **Requirements Engineering** – Defining what the system must accomplish.
    
- **System Design & Architecture** – Structuring components and interfaces.
    
- **Implementation & Integration** – Writing, testing, and combining code.
    
- **Quality Assurance & Testing** – Verifying reliability and correctness.
    
- **Maintenance & Evolution** – Updating and improving existing systems.
    
- **Project Management & Methodologies** – Organizing development using approaches like Agile, Scrum, or Waterfall.

---
### `Core Technologies`

- **Version Control Systems** – Git, GitHub, GitLab
    
- **Programming Languages** – Python, Java, C#, JavaScript, SQL
    
- **Frameworks** – React, Node.js, Spring Boot, Django
    
- **Build & CI/CD Tools** – Jenkins, GitHub Actions, Docker, Kubernetes
    
- **Testing Frameworks** – JUnit, PyTest, Selenium
    
- **Project Management Tools** – Jira, Trello, Azure Boards
