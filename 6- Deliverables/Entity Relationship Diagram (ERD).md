
### `Definition`

An Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) visually represents the structure of a database, showing entities, their attributes, and the relationships between them. It is used to design and communicate the logical data model.

---
### `Key Ideas`

- Defines database tables (entities) and their fields (attributes).
    
- Shows relationships such as one-to-one, one-to-many, and many-to-many.
    
- Helps build normalized, consistent data models.
    
- Supports both high-level conceptual design and detailed schema planning.
    
- Commonly used before database implementation.
