
### `Definition`

The **Product Backlog** is an **ordered, dynamic list of all desired work** on the product â€” a single source of truth for everything that might improve the product.  It evolves continuously as new ideas, needs, and feedback emerge.

---
### `Key Ideas`

- Owned and prioritized by the **Product Owner**.
    
- Contains **User Stories**, **Epics**, and **defects** representing business or technical needs.
    
- Continuously refined through **backlog refinement (grooming)** sessions.
    
- Prioritization is often based on **business value**, **risk**, and **dependencies**.
    
- Associated commitment: **Product Goal**.
