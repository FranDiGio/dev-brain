
### `Definition`

Virtual machines that run on **multi-tenant hardware**, where physical servers are **shared across multiple customers** through virtualization.

---
### `Key Ideas`

- Default model for most cloud VMs.
    
- Provides **logical isolation** even when sharing physical hardware.
    
- Most **cost-efficient** and suitable for general workloads.
