
### `Definition`

The **Sprint Backlog** is a **subset of the Product Backlog** selected for a single sprint, along with a concrete plan for delivering it.  
It represents the work the team commits to completing within the sprint timeframe.

---
### `Key Ideas`

- Owned by the **Scrum Team** â€” specifically the Developers.
    
- Includes:
    
    - Selected **Product Backlog items** (stories, tasks, etc.)
        
    - A **Sprint Goal** (the unifying objective)
        
    - A **plan** for delivering the work.
    
- Continuously updated during the sprint as progress is made.
    
- Provides visibility into sprint progress via tools like **burndown charts**.
