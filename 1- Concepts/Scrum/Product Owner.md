
### `Definition`

The **Product Owner** is responsible for **defining the product vision**, **managing the product backlog**, and **maximizing value delivery**. They serve as the **bridge between stakeholders and the Scrum Team**, ensuring the team builds the right product.

---
### `Key Ideas`

- **Vision & Direction:** Clearly articulates the product vision and goals.
    
- **Backlog Management:** Prioritizes, refines, and maintains backlog items for clarity and sprint readiness.
    
- **Stakeholder Liaison:** Represents customer and business needs, eliminating direct stakeholder interruptions.
    
- **Decision Authority:** Accepts or rejects product increments and decides whether to pivot or persevere.
