
### `Definition`

An **Increment** is the **sum of all completed Product Backlog items** that meet the **Definition of Done** at the end of a sprint.  It represents a **usable, potentially shippable product** that adds tangible value to the overall product goal.

---
### `Key Ideas`

- Each sprint produces **at least one Increment**.
    
- Increments are **additive** â€” each builds on all previous work.
    
- Multiple increments can be created during a single sprint.
    
- Must meet the **Definition of Done** to ensure quality and consistency.
    
- Associated commitment: **Definition of Done**.
