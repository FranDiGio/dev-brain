
### `Definition`

The **production environment** is the live system used by real users. It prioritizes stability, security, performance, and availability above all else.

---
### `Key Ideas`

- **Live User Traffic:**  
    Any failure directly impacts users.
    
- **High Reliability:**  
    Monitoring, alerting, backups, and redundancy are critical.
    
- **Strict Controls:**  
    Changes follow approval and deployment processes.
    
- **Real Data:**  
    Uses actual user and business data.
    
- **Performance & Security:**  
    Optimized for scalability, latency, and protection.
