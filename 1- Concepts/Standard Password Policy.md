
### `Definition`

A **Standard Password Policy** defines the **rules and best practices** governing how passwords are created, stored, and maintained to prevent unauthorized access.

---
### `Key Ideas`

- **Complexity Requirements:**  
    Specifies minimum length, character types, and disallows weak or reused passwords.
    
- **Lifecycle Management:**  
    Enforces expiration periods, password history, and resets for compromised accounts.
    
- **Multi-Factor Integration:**  
    Often paired with MFA for stronger identity assurance.
    
- **Compliance Role:**  
    Required for frameworks like NIST 800-63, ISO 27001, and PCI-DSS.
