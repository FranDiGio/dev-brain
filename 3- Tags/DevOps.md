
## `Definition`

The term **(development and operations)** is an extension of agile development environments that aims to enhance the process of software delivery as a whole.

## `Key Ideas`

- Not working in silos (Dev & Ops working separately)
- Fail fast and rollback quickly
- Test in market instead of analyzing (A/B Testing)
- Comes from a change of culture, not tools or procedures
- Openness, trust, communication, and transparency

##### `Requires`

- A change in culture
- New application design
- Leveraging automation
- Programmable platform to CI/CD
